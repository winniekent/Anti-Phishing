Most common healthcare phishing signals

These patterns are typical in spoofed medical and insurance communications.

Look-alike sender

Fake domains or names that mimic your clinic, insurer, or patient portal.

Urgent medical demand

Claims of missed appointments, overdue bills, or urgent test results to force quick action.

Portal phishing

Links that look like patient portal URLs but redirect to credential-harvesting pages.

Unsafe attachments

Unexpected medical forms, invoices, or reports asking for passwords or payment details.
